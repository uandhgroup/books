<p className="grey">You can get different types of ethernet cables.</p>

### Cable Types

`-` Coaxial Cable <br />
`-` Twisted Pair <br />
`├──` Catgory 3 – Three twists per inch <br />
`├──` Cat 5 – five twists per inch <br />
`├──` Cat 5e – 5 twists per square inch; pairs also twisted together <br />
`├──` Cat 6 – Six twist per inch, with improved insulation <br />
`└──` RJ45 connector is used to connect the device. <br />
`-` Fibre Optic <br />

### Characteristics
`-` Routing IP packets <br />
`-` Quality of Service classification <br />
`-` Virtual LAN configurations <br />
`-` Cheap <br />

Data collisions can occur is two devices send at the same time especially if a hub is used.

CSMA/CD protects against this

### CSMA/CD
<p className="grey">Carrier Sense Multiple Access with Collision Detection</p>

If data collision occurs senders back off and resend. It uses carrier-sensing to defer transmissions until no other stations are transmitting
