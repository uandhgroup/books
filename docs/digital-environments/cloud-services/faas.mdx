<p className="grey">Function as a service</p>


`-` Functions are client managed and deployed on the cloud <br />
`-` Functions are executed on demand in pay as you go fasion (scale up or down) <br />
`-` Referred to as `Serverless computing` <br />
`-` Network infrastructure is vendor managed <br />
