<p className="grey">Resilience - Strong, recover, overcome problems.</p>

The process of reducing vulnerabilities in a digital environment by improving security, lowering risks and providing methods of recovery, should threats develop into real incidents.

### Creating a Resilient Digital Environment

`-` Installation of software updates/upgrades <br />
`-` Replacement and removal of hardware <br />
`-` Add redundancy to systems <br />
`-` Decommission and remove legacy hardware and software <br />
`-` Device hardening <br />
`-` Maintain effective back up systems <br />
`-` Appropriate and reviewed Standard Operating Procedures (SPOs) <br />
`-` Structure staff training <br />
