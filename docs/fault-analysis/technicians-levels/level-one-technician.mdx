## Tasks of a Level 1 Support technician

`-` Filters the requests <br />
`-` Basic troubleshooting (password reset, printer configuration, ticket routing & escalation to level 2 and 3 <br />
`-` Gathers and analyses information about the issue and finds the best way to resolve it. <br />
`-` Documents the calls and tickets received <br />
`-` Routine Checks <br />
`-` Maintains and updates the record keeping software <br />
`-` Assists with tracking inventory and assets <br />
`-` Assist with development and rollout of new technologies and systems <br />
`-` Assist with installation <br />
`-` Perform remote troubleshooting <br />
