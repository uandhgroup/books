<p className="grey">A hazard and a risk are two different things.</p>

### Hazard
A hazard is anything that has potential to cause harm. <br />
This could be bare wire, trailling cable or wet floor. 

### Risk
What cold happen due to a hazard. <br />
For example: Trailing cable (hazard) trip over it (risk).
