<p className="grey">A professional body, that represents those working in systems analysis, design, programming and implementation of computer systems in the UK and internationally.</p>

They say they want members to conduct themselves as professionals at all times, improve their professionalism, not to misuse their expertise by manipulating other and to not make public statements unless competent to do so.
