<p className="grey">An Act to make provision about the interception of communications, equipment interference and the acquisition and retention of communications data, bulk personal datasets.</p>

## Main reasons for creating an act

`-` To give certain group legal rights to carry out digital surveillance and access digital communication held by a person or organisation. <br />
`-` Needed to keep up to date with present technology. <br />
`-` Brings together all powers already available to law enforcement to obtain data about communications <br />
`-` Make the powers and safeguards clear and understandable <br />
`-` Overhauls the way the powers were overseen <br />
