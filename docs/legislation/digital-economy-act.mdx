<p className="grey">The act addresses policy issues related to electronic communications infrastructure and services, and updates the conditions for and sentencing of criminal copyright infringement.</p>

The UK's digital economy is growing fast and digital technology is transforming every sector and all aspects of our lives. <br />
If the UK is to remain ahead and be a world leader in digital economy we need to raise ambition and the Digital Economy Act includes a range of measures to support this. <br />

<br />

This act: <br />
`-` Makes the minimum standard of broadband connectivity to be 10 megabits per second <br />
`-` Empowers consumers and provide better connectivity so that everyone has access to broadband <br />
`-` Build a better infrastructure fit for the digital age <br />
`-` Enable better public services using digital technologies <br />
`-` Provide important protections for citizens from spam email and nuisance calls <br />
