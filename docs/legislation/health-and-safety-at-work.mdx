<p className="grey">This act protects staff at work</p>

Primary piece of legislation covering occupational health ad safety in the UK. <br />


## It sets out general duties which...

`-` Employers have towards employees and members of the public <br />
`-` Employees have to themselves and each other <br />
`-` Certain self employed have towards themselves and others <br />

### Enabling act
The enabling act is a piece of legislation which a body grants the power to take certain actions. <br />
An enabling act allows further laws (regulations) to be made without the need to pass another act. <br />
Some regulations apply across all industries. (e.g. manual handling)

### Regulations

`-` Manual handling operations regulations <br />
`-` Work at height regulations <br />
`-` Health and safety (display screen equipment DSE) regulations <br />
`-` Management of heath and safety at work regulations <br />
`-` Provision and use of work equipment regulations <br />
