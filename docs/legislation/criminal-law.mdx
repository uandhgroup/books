<p className="grey">The criminal law sets out the definitions of criminal offences and the rules and procedures that apply when the police investigate an offence they think you've committed. <br /> It is a system of laws concerned with punishment of individuals who commit crimes.</p>

### Functions
<code className="greennumbercode">Maintain order</code> Criminal law provides predictability, letting people know what to expect from others. Without criminal law, there would be chaos and uncertainty.

<br />

<br />

<code className="greennumbercode">Resolving disputes</code> The law makes it possible to resolve conflicts and disputes between quarrelling citizens. It provides peaceful, orderly wat to handle grievances.

<br />

<br />

<code className="greennumbercode">Protecting individuals and property</code> Criminal law protects citizens from criminals who would inflict physical harm on others and take their possessions.

<br />

<br />

<code className="greennumbercode">Providing for smooth functioning of society</code> Criminal law enables the government to collect taxes, control pollution and accomplish other socially beneficial tasks.

<br />

<br />

<code className="greennumbercode">Safeguarding civil liberties:</code> Criminal law protects individual rights.
