Supermarkets have customer loyalty cards to collect customers shopping behaviour data. <br />

## Benefits to the business

`-` It ensures that the customers keep coming back and boosts customer retention. <br />
`-` It helps businesses compete for customers by offering better loyalty card schemes <br />
`-` Creates a better experience for customers when they get a discount <br />
`-` Allows businesses to communicate with their customers better <br />
`-` They can advertise and market products to certain people who may appreciate it more <br />
`-` Stock certain products in different location, based on market research <br />
