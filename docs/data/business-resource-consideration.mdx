Business resource considerations for data entry and maintence. 


**Things to take into account in terms of placing he data onto the system in the first place:**

## Operational
`-` Time - Data might change, length of time to enter the data. <br />
`-` Staffing

## Financial
`-` Budget - Cost of staffing, hardware/software costs <br />
`-` Estimating and forecasting - Issues with the data, and keeping it up to date

## Technological
`-` Hardware - Equiptment - eg: servers, computer components... <br />
`-` Software - What software, use of software, training... <br />
`-` Storage - Amount of storage, softs, onsite storage, cloud, backup of data...
